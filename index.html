Art is often considered the process or product of deliberately arranging elements in a way that appeals to the senses or emotions. It encompasses a diverse range of human activities, creations and ways of expression, including music, literature, film, sculpture, painting and photography.
Art Photography is photography created in line with the vision of photographer as artist, using photography as a medium for creative expression. In this photography vision of an artist behind the camera is presented rather than the objective reality of whatever subjectthe camera is pointed at.
